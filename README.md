## 📊 GlobalMart Sales Performance Analysis

### Business Problem

- This report provides a comprehensive analysis of GlobalMart's sales performance across regions, product categories, and product sub-categories from 2014 to 2017. However, challenges such as inconsistent sales trends, regional performance differences, and the impact of discounts on profitability hinder its ability to make data-driven decisions.
- The goal is to derive actionable insights from sales data, enabling improved revenue growth , operational efficiency, and strategic decision-making.
- The company wanted to know the following;
- What products had the highest sales and profit?
- What products had the least sales and profits?
- What time of the year and month were sales and profits the highest?
- Which region and state had the highest sales and the made the most profit?
- What product category, subcategories and product name were most and least bought according to region and state?

### Dataset

This dataset was created for educational purposes to  derive actionable insights from sales data, enabling improved revenue growth , operational efficiency, and strategic decision-making.. It includes region, profits, product categories, product sub categories, transaction, and sales.

- The dataset was cleaned and below was the income statments used to derive analysis from

Total  transactions                                   556k

### Tools

-PowerBI

### Process

- Data Cleaning
- Data Transformation
- Analysis

### Key Insights

-The GlobalMart Sales is profitable. 
-Sales Trends: The West region generates the highest revenue and high profit margins compared to other like the south region has both low profit margins and low total revenue.
-Product Performance: Technology products have the highest profitability, while Furniture has
lower margins despite high sales and Office Supplies leads in both sales and profit.
-Regional Analysis: The East region shows potential for growth but currently underperforms
in terms of both sales and profit. West region excels in profit and sales but lower orders
compared to the East.
-Trends over time: December generates the highest sales compared to other months whereas
January has highest profit margin.


### Recommendations

 -It is recommended to negotiate lower raw material costs to improve total profit .
-Discounts should be reduced so as to increase the profits.
-Focus marketing efforts on high-margin products like Technology to maximize
profitability.
-Optimize inventory and reduce discounts for Furniture to improve profit margins.
-Increase investment in the East region to unlock growth opportunities.
-Increase products manufactured during December since the sales are high during this
period.
-Review discounts offered in December as it affects the profit margins, sales are high but
profits are very low.
-Increase marketing in regions with high profit margin and low sales to boost overall
performance.
